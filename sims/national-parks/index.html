    US National Parks¶

    An interactive map exploring America's national parks - protected natural treasures across the country.

    Run Full Screen MicroSim

    About This MicroSim¶

    Discover America's national parks by type:

    • Mountain Parks - Yellowstone, Yosemite, Rocky Mountain
    • Canyons/Desert - Grand Canyon, Death Valley, Arches
    • Forests - Redwood, Sequoia, Olympic
    • Coastal - Acadia, Everglades, Channel Islands
    • Volcanic - Hawaii Volcanoes, Crater Lake

    Features¶

    • Click parks to learn unique facts
    • Quiz mode to test your knowledge
    • Filter by park type
