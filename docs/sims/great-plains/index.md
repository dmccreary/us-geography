## About This MicroSim

Explore America's breadbasket through an elevation profile:

- See how elevation changes from mountains to river
- Understand why the Great Plains are excellent for farming
- Learn about the geography that shaped American agriculture

## Learning Objectives

After using this simulation, students will understand:

- The gradual slope from the Rockies eastward
- Why the Great Plains are called "America's Breadbasket"
- The relationship between geography and agriculture
