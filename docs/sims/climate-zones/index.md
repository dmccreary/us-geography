## About This MicroSim

Explore America's climate diversity:

- **Subarctic** - Alaska's cold northern climate
- **Humid Continental** - Northeast and Midwest seasons
- **Humid Subtropical** - Southeast warmth
- **Semi-Arid** - Western dry lands
- **Desert** - Southwest heat
- **Mediterranean** - California's mild climate
- **Marine West Coast** - Pacific Northwest moisture
- **Tropical** - Hawaii and southern Florida

## Features

- Click regions to learn about each climate
- Quiz mode to test your knowledge
