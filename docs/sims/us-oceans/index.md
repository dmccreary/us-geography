## About This MicroSim

Learn about America's ocean borders:

- **Atlantic Ocean** - East coast, from Maine to Florida
- **Pacific Ocean** - West coast, plus Alaska and Hawaii
- **Gulf of Mexico** - Southern coast, from Florida to Texas
- **Arctic Ocean** - Northern Alaska coast

- Click oceans to learn interesting facts
