## About This MicroSim

Explore America's transportation infrastructure:

- **Interstate Highways** - The road network connecting states
- **Railroads** - Freight and passenger rail lines
- **Airports** - Major aviation hubs
- **Ports** - Coastal and inland shipping centers

## Features

- Filter by transportation type
- Click hubs to learn about their importance
- Quiz mode to test your knowledge
