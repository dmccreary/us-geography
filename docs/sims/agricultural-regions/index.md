## About This MicroSim

Explore America's breadbasket and agricultural zones:

- **Corn Belt** - The Midwest's golden harvest
- **Wheat Belt** - Great Plains grain production
- **Cotton Belt** - Southern farming heritage
- **Dairy Belt** - Northern dairy farms
- **Fruit/Vegetables** - Specialty crop regions

## Features

- Filter by region type (Grain Belts, Other Crops)
- Click regions for detailed crop information
- Quiz mode to test your knowledge
