An interactive map exploring the region where warm and cold air masses collide to create America's most tornado-prone area.

## About This MicroSim

Understand how geography creates tornado conditions:

- **Warm Gulf Air** - Moist air from the Gulf of Mexico
- **Cold Canadian Air** - Dry air from Canada
- **Collision Zone** - Where air masses meet and tornadoes form

## Features

- Animate air mass movements
- Click states to learn about tornado activity
- Quiz mode to test your knowledge
