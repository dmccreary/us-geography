## About This MicroSim

Discover America's most iconic landmarks:

- **Monuments** - Statue of Liberty, Lincoln Memorial, and more
- **Bridges** - Golden Gate Bridge and other engineering marvels
- **Historic Sites** - Places where history was made
- **Natural Wonders** - Grand Canyon, Yellowstone, and more

## Features

- Click landmarks to learn interesting facts
- Quiz mode to test your knowledge
- Filter by landmark type
