# Rivers and Lakes of the United States

An interactive map showing the major water features across America - rivers and lakes that shaped the nation.

## About This MicroSim

Explore America's water features:

**Rivers**: Mississippi, Missouri, Colorado, Columbia, Ohio
**Lakes**: The five Great Lakes plus Great Salt Lake

## How to Use

1. **Toggle** Rivers or Lakes buttons to show/hide features
2. **Click** on any river or lake to see information
3. **Quiz Me!** to test your water features knowledge

## Key Concepts Demonstrated

- Rivers flow from mountains to oceans or larger rivers
- The Great Lakes hold 21% of Earth's fresh surface water
- Water features were crucial for transportation and settlement

## Lesson Plan

**Learning Objective:** Students will identify major US rivers and lakes and describe their locations.

**Bloom's Taxonomy Level:** Remember/Understand (L1/L2)

**Activities:**

1. **Explore Rivers (5 min)**: Click each river and trace its path
2. **Find the Lakes (5 min)**: Use the HOMES acronym to find all five Great Lakes
3. **Quiz (8 min)**: Answer 6 questions about US water features
