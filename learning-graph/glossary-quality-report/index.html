    "The second longest river in North America, flowing from Minnesota to the Gulf of Mexico." Example: The Mississippi River is so important that it's sometimes called "America's River."

    • Precise: Accurately describes the river
    • Concise: 15 words
    • Distinctive: No confusion with other rivers
    • Non-circular: Uses simpler terms (river, North America)

    Latitude

    "Imaginary lines that run east and west around Earth, measuring distance north or south of the equator." Example: The equator is at 0 degrees latitude, and Minnesota is around 45 degrees north latitude.

    • Precise: Correctly explains the concept
    • Concise: 17 words
    • Distinctive: Clearly different from longitude
    • Non-circular: Does not use the term being defined
